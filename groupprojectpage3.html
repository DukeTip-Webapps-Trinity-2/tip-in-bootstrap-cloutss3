In your classes you will explore lots of cool stuff which is really interesting. There is a class from 9-12, then a break for lunch, then afternoon class from 1-4. There is also an evening study from 6-7 where you talk about logistical things and also work. I know that sounds bad but there is no homework and the classes are interesting.

Your classes are really fun and interesting and they will be a high point in your day. You will learn subjects that you don't normally get in school. You will be at state-of-the-art facilities on a really nice college campus. Everything is great and you will love it.
